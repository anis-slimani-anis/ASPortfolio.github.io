About

A bit about
me.

I'm Anis Slimani, a product designer based in Paris. I recently finished my MSc in Human Computer Interaction at Université Paris-Saclay, alongside a one year contract at Legalstart where I worked on real product problems with real constraints.

I like working on things that are genuinely complex: complex flows, dense information, products where getting the details wrong actually matters. I approach design with research and structure, not just intuition.

I also write frontend code, which means I understand what's realistic to build and I can prototype things properly rather than just wireframing them.

Currently looking for my next role, ideally in a product team where design is taken seriously.
